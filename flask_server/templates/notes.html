Welcome to your personal Dashboard

January 10, 2025 by Me

Use the options in the slide menu to access various managers for your online life.The toolbar on the left can be used to add or remove entries from the databases that store your information in the database.

The database is encryptable, and it is encrypted by default. Should you desire it, opt for it to be unencrypted by going into the site-settings.config file menu on the slide out menu.

The various managers provided here include:

  • A bookmark manager
  • A password manager
  • A note manager
